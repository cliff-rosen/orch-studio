Company Name: Orchestrator Studios

Tagline: Building reliable, production-grade AI products for specialized verticals.

Description:

Orchestrator Studios is an AI product studio that develops systematic methodologies to overcome the well-known limitations of LLMs — hallucinations, reliability gaps, context constraints — and partners with domain experts to embed real-world industry knowledge into research and productivity tools.

Most AI products fail in production because they treat LLMs as magic. We're built around a different premise: that getting AI to work in specialized, high-stakes domains requires methodology, not just models. We identify the failure modes, build systems that work around them, and make sure the output is trustworthy enough to act on.

Our portfolio includes Knowledge Horizon (AI-powered scientific literature monitoring for life sciences and legal defense) and TableThat (chat-driven structured data for analysts and researchers).
