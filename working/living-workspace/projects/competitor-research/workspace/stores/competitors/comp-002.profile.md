# Asana — Competitor Profile

## One-liner
The mature, enterprise-friendly work management platform. Spans projects, OKRs, workflows, and reporting for cross-functional teams.

## Positioning
Mid-to-enterprise work management. Generalist by design — supports many work patterns. Public company with ~$700M ARR.

## Product strengths
- Broadest functional surface area (projects, portfolios, OKRs, workflows)
- Strong cross-functional fit; not dev-specific
- Mature enterprise features: SOC 2, SAML, audit logs, compliance
- Established marketplace and integration ecosystem

## Product gaps
- Sluggish for fast-moving teams compared to Linear
- Complexity and configurability can overwhelm newer or smaller teams
- Less developer-friendly (API exists but not the focus)
- Pricing scales aggressively with seats

## What we'd compete on
- Speed and lighter cognitive overhead
- Specific opinions about how work flows (vs. Asana's "configure it however")
- Pricing for small-to-mid teams
- Modern UX
