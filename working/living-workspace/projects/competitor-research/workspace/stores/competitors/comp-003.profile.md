# ClickUp — Competitor Profile

## One-liner
"One app to replace them all." Aggressively horizontal work platform that bundles tasks, docs, whiteboards, chat, and goals.

## Positioning
Mid-market generalist. Marketing-led growth via "free forever" + aggressive feature accumulation. Aimed at SMBs and growing mid-market teams.

## Product strengths
- Massive feature surface — almost certainly does what the prospect needs
- Aggressive free tier and value pricing
- Fast feature shipping cadence
- Broad integration support

## Product gaps
- Notorious complexity and visual noise; "doing everything" comes at a UX cost
- Performance and reliability complaints persist
- Lacks the focus of Linear (for software) or Asana (for enterprise process)
- Steeper onboarding than competitors

## What we'd compete on
- Focused, opinionated workflow vs. ClickUp's "everything"
- UX clarity and performance
- Reliable execution on a smaller surface area
